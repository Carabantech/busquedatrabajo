# Story Bank -- Master STAR+R Stories

This file accumulates your best interview stories over time. Each evaluation
(Block F) can add new stories here. Instead of memorizing many answers,
maintain 5-10 deep stories that you can adapt to common behavioral questions.

## How it works

1. Every time `/career-ops oferta` generates Block F (Interview Plan), new STAR+R stories can be appended here.
2. Before an interview, review this file to reuse strong stories.
3. The "Big Three" questions can often be answered from this bank:
   - "Tell me about yourself"
   - "Tell me about your most impactful project"
   - "Tell me about a conflict you resolved"
